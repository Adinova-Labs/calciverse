What is EMI Calculator?

EMI Calculator is an online financial tool that helps you calculate the Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) for your loan. It instantly shows your monthly payment amount, total interest payable, and total repayment amount based on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.

How EMI is Calculated?

EMI is calculated using a standard mathematical formula:

EMI Formula:
EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] ÷ [(1+R)^N – 1]

Where:
P = Loan Amount
R = Monthly Interest Rate
N = Loan Tenure in Months

Benefits of Using EMI Calculator

  • Instant and accurate EMI results
  • Helps compare different loan offers
  • Shows total interest payable
  • Useful for home loan, car loan, and personal loan

Frequently Asked Questions

Does EMI remain fixed?
Yes, in fixed interest loans EMI remains constant throughout the tenure.

Can I reduce my EMI?
Yes, by increasing tenure or negotiating a lower interest rate.

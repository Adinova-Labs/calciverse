Estimate your mutual fund investment growth with systematic monthly investments.

How SIP Works

SIP allows investors to invest small amounts monthly. Returns are compounded over time, helping build wealth systematically.

SIP Formula

M = P × ((1 + r)^n − 1) × (1 + r) / r

Where P is monthly investment, r is monthly interest rate and n is total months.

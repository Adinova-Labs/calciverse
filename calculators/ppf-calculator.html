About PPF Calculator

The Public Provident Fund (PPF) is one of India's most popular long-term investment schemes backed by the Government of India. Our PPF calculator helps you calculate maturity amount, total investment and interest earned instantly.

How PPF Interest is Calculated

PPF interest is compounded annually and announced by the Government of India every quarter. This calculator assumes yearly compounding for accurate long-term projection.

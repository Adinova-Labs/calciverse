Frequently Asked Questions

How is mortgage calculated?

A mortgage is calculated using the amortization formula that considers loan amount, interest rate and loan term to determine monthly payments.

What is included in a mortgage payment?

A typical mortgage payment includes principal and interest. Some loans may also include property tax and insurance.

Can I use this mortgage calculator for INR?

Yes. This calculator supports USD, EUR, GBP and INR currencies.

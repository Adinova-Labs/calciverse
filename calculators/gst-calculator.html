What is GST Calculator?

GST Calculator is an online financial tool that helps you calculate Goods and Services Tax in India. It allows you to quickly add GST to a base amount or remove GST from a gross amount. The calculator also automatically splits the GST amount into CGST and SGST for easy tax breakdown.

How to Use GST Calculator?

  • Enter the original amount.
  • Select the GST rate (5%, 12%, 18%, 28% or custom).
  • Choose whether you want to add or remove GST.
  • Click Calculate to get instant results.

GST Slabs in India

India follows multiple GST slabs including 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%. Most goods and services fall under the 18% category. The calculator supports all major slabs along with custom rates.

Formula Used in GST Calculation

Adding GST:
GST Amount = (Original Amount × GST Rate) ÷ 100

Removing GST:
Base Amount = Gross Amount ÷ (1 + GST Rate/100)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is CGST and SGST?
CGST is Central GST collected by the central government, and SGST is State GST collected by the state government.

Can I use this calculator for custom GST rates?
Yes, you can enter any custom GST percentage for accurate calculation.
